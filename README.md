## üé® Customization

### Personal Information
Update the following files with your information:
- `src/components/Hero.js` - Name, roles, and description
- `src/components/About.js` - About section content
- `src/components/SocialLinks.js` - Social media links and stats
- `src/components/Contact.js` - Contact information

### Colors and Styling
- `tailwind.config.js` - Custom colors and animations
- `src/index.css` - Additional custom styles

### Content
- `src/components/Skills.js` - Your skills and technologies
- `src/components/Projects.js` - Your projects and portfolio items
- `src/components/Achievements.js` - Awards and recognitions
- `src/components/Education.js` - Educational background
- `src/components/Certifications.js` - Professional certifications
